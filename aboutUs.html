Introduction To Nanjing Narong Computer Hanzi Technology Co.Ltd.


Nanjing Narong Computer Hanzi Technology Co., Ltd. is a newly established network operation company, wholly owned by Narong (Nanjing) Cultural Diffusion Technology Co., Ltd. and based on the original invention patent, which specializes in the information infrastructure of the internationalization of Chinese language under the background of the Internet and promotes the concept of "Dynamic Independent Chinese Learning" （namely Learning Chinese Anywhere & Anytime）.


To thicken the broth further, the guiding ideology of the establishment of Nanjing Narong Computer Hanzi Technology Co., Ltd. is to realize equal "Computer National Treatment" in Keylearning and Keywriting for Chinese characters based on keyboard, which is the best tool for man-machine dialogue, so as to enable overseas Chinese learners in the global "Chinese Popular" to make the "dynamic learning" of the informationization of Chinese language be unified with their future paperless office application. So that they can obtain the convenience of learning and using Chinese with computer as easily as learning and using English and German with computer, and solve the problem that compared to those Chinese with the same number of Chinese characters, foreigners without the help from the persons understanding Chinese around cannot naturally accumulate the large number of phrases required for high-speed Pinyin typing, which is the main reason for their long-term low ability to KEYWRITE for Chinese character (which is also the important reason why Professor Gu Anda, a Sinologist at the University of Gottingen in Germany, appealed to Confucius Institute to attach importance to the cultivation of keywriting ability of overseas Chinese learners).


The purpose of Nanjing Narong Computer Hanzi Technology Co., Ltd. is to achieve the equal "Computer National Treatment" in the field of Chinese learning and Chinese application through technical means, so as to effectively change the long-standing phenomenon of the much lower rate of Chinese mastering behind the "Chinese Popular" compared with that of phonetic languages around the world. And as a result, in the virtual world of the information internet age it can clear up the most basic technical barriers in the future for "Chinese Language Dream", which would make it keep abreast with the application level of English.


In order to avoid “making cars behind closed doors”, Nanjing Narong Computer Hanzi Technology Co., Ltd. has made a down-to-earth market exploration through two ways for the foreign students who use English as the teaching language in their major courses in China : one is to teach themselves the Easy Chinese Code ™ (ECCode ™) independently, and the other is face-to-face teaching. The results are encouraging. The former was able to master the basic principles of the ECCode™ on the basis of their surviving knowledge of Chinese characters, although most of the knowledge of Chinese characters was returned to their Chinese teachers because of using English as the teaching language in their professional courses; The latter, with the same background, also fully grasped the basic principles of the ECCode™ in less than 40 minutes of a lecture. For this reason, the ease of learning of the ECCode™ has also been confirmed in this process. This is a subjective effort of Nanjing Narong Computer Hanzi Technology Co., Ltd. to completely eradicate the rich self-consciousness of general technology companies which easily make them fall into the situation of closed-door car-making, and also an attempt to prove itself in practice.


Considering that the Chinese character learning threshold has been lowered by the Easy Chinese Code™ (ECCode™), the efficiency of Chinese teaching in Colleges and universities can be greatly improved, and more time saved by the ECCode™ for Chinese teachers can be spent on the cultivation of other Chinese abilities of international students, in order to help improve the informationization level of Chinese character teaching in universities and fill the gaps in keywriting ability training, Nanjing Narong Computer Hanzi Technology Co., Ltd. and Narong (Nanjing) Cultural Diffusion Technology Co., Ltd. have jointly launched the matched training platform of "Chinese Keywriting School". It provides technical means for foreign students to continuously consolidate their knowledge of Chinese characters by means of paperless homework after they enter the professional learning stage.


In view of the fact that there are fewer than 500,000 foreign students in the world who have the opportunity to study in China, and the number of foreigners in the global "Chinese Popular" is reported to be as high as 150 million, and in the foreseeable future, it is reported that there will be 200 million foreign people engaging in learning Chinese, that is to say, 99.99% of foreigners will be in the state of learning Chinese in the absence of Chinese environment in the future, so Nanjing Narong Computer Hanzi Technology Co., Ltd. is entrusted with the mission of the enterprise to hope to achieve the full coverage of this Keylearning & Keywriting Method for Chinese characters in the global Chinese corner through the operation of the network platform and continuous perseverant efforts over time.


Nanjing Narong Computer Hanzi Technology Co., Ltd. aims to make foreigners truly feel: “Chinese Power Made Easy！”.

南京纳融计算机汉字技术有限公司简介


南京纳融计算机汉字技术有限公司是由纳融（南京）文化传播科技有限公司全资控股的、基于独创发明专利而新创建的、专门致力于互联网背景下汉语国际化的信息基础设施建设的一家推动“汉语动态自主学习”理念（Learning Chinese Anywhere & Anytime）的网络运营公司。


具体地说，南京纳融计算机汉字技术有限公司的创建指导思想就是要基于键盘这个人机对话的最佳工具，努力实现汉字键学（keylearning）与键写（keywriting）方面的平等“电脑国民待遇”，以便让全球“汉语热”中的海外汉语学习者能够把汉语信息化的“动态学习”与未来的无纸化办公应用统一起来，从而获得像学习和使用英语、德语那样方便地获得学习和使用汉语的便利性，并借此解决基于其汉语环境缺失所造成的、外国人难以在短期内自然积累出具有相同识字数量的中国人所具备的那种高速拼音打字所需的庞大词组数量——这是他们的汉字键写能力长期低下的主要原因（这也是德国哥廷根大学汉学家顾安达教授呼吁孔子学院重视海外汉语学习者键写能力培养的原因之所在）。


南京纳融计算机汉字技术有限公司的宗旨，就是要通过技术手段实现汉语学习与汉语应用的平等“电脑国民待遇”，以便切实改变“汉语热”景象背后相较于拼音语言的汉语“学成率”长期不彰的现象，并以此为汉语在信息化互联网时代的虚拟世界里能够在未来并驾齐驱于英语的“中国语言梦”扫清最基本的技术障碍。


为了避免闭门造车，南京纳融计算机汉字技术有限公司针对在华使用英语上专业课的外国留学生分别做了脚踏实地的“独立自学”Easy Chinese Code (ECCode)与当面讲授的市场摸底，结果令人备受鼓舞。前者尽管目前使用英语上专业课而把大多数的汉字知识还给了汉语老师，但也能够基于其尚存的汉字知识自学掌握ECCode（【汉易码】）的基本原理；而具有同样背景的后者则在不到40分钟的讲座当中同样地完全掌握了ECCode的基本原理。为此，ECCode的易学性也在此过程当中得到了印证。这是南京纳融计算机汉字技术有限公司想要摒弃一般技术型公司自我意识浓郁而容易陷入闭门造车境地的一种主观努力，也是寄望在实践中证明自己的一种尝试。


考虑到Easy Chinese Code (ECCode，【汉易码】)降低了汉字学习的门槛，可以大大提高高校的汉语教学效率，并让汉语教师们可以把节省出来的更多时间用于汉语其他能力的培养方面，南京纳融计算机汉字技术有限公司与纳融（南京）文化传播科技有限公司联袂推出了与之配套的“Chinese Keywriting School”实训平台，以便在辅助提升高校对外汉字教学的信息化水平和填补键写能力培养空白的同时，也为留学生进入专业学习阶段之后借助于“无纸化作业”而不断地巩固其在入学时所学的汉字知识提供了技术手段。


鉴于全球有机会来华留学的外国学生只有不到五十万人，而全球“汉语热”中的外国人据报道已高达1.5亿，在可预见的未来据报道更可达2亿人口，也就是说未来将有99.99%的外国人会处于汉语环境缺失的汉语学习状态，南京纳融计算机汉字技术有限公司所被赋予的企业使命就是要通过网络化平台的运营，希望通过日积月累的不懈坚持，最终能够实现这种汉字键学与键写方法在全球汉语角落的全覆盖。


南京纳融计算机汉字技术有限公司的企业目标就是要让外国人真切地感受到：“汉语能力获得变得容易了！”.
